About

We are an ed-tech company solving the Skill Gap problem in engineering. We have a multi-pronged approach to solve the problem and have demonstrated success in the last five years. We bring all the stakeholders - Colleges, Companies, and Students together and bridge the gap that exists among the players.

Lycaeum was established in 2013 in Kozhikode, Kerala to foster technical excellence among engineering students. It has been the space where programmers are created through Guidance, Mentoring & Placement.

3 steps to get a job

Guidance

An expert touch to cement your programming career

The guidance program is designed to upskill students while they’re still in college. A live course using a mix of online and offline methodology, the program creates ready to deploy graduates who are trained not only with the latest industry skills, but also best in class language and professional skills

Guidance provides

  • Technical and soft skills development
  • Daily quizzes and sessions to practice solving real problems
  • Study circles and live engagement
  • Weekly interaction with the technical and Soft skill experts
  • Customized course material
  • Assessment & feedback

Mentoring

A Leg up to find a software Job

The mentoring program is Lycaeum’s flagship project. Since 2013, the program has helped around 50 students to break into the industry. An industry expert mentors the students in a class-room setting working with the students on hands-on problems slowly helping them build the expertise required to function fully in the industry

Mentoring provides

  • Programming skills
  • Python proficiency
  • Web dev and basic DevOps
  • Collaborative projects
  • Code comprehension
  • Placement assistance

Placement

Be ready to get placed

Proven track record of 95% + students placed in reputed companies through Lycaeum. There is a constant demand for Lycaeum graduates in the industry as they are well versed in the necessary skills to function at the highest level

The placement program has two offerings

  • After the guidance and mentoring program, we assist the ready to deploy students to associated companies for Internship or Job. We are constantly adding more companies to our network
  • An automated placement system that has been deployed at major institutions that enable the entire placement process to be automated. Colleges and students are already benefiting from the software at various institutions
